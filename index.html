About Us

Located in the heart of Bhiria City, Sindh, Star Academy Public School Bhiria City is committed to providing quality education that fosters intellectual curiosity, character, and civic responsibility. Our teachers focus on individualized attention, ensuring every student thrives academically and socially.

Academics

We offer a structured learning path from early years through middle school.

Nursery & KG

Early childhood learning with play-based, phonics-ready curriculum.

  • Social & emotional development
  • Foundational literacy and numeracy

Primary (1 - 5)

Core subjects with enrichment activities and regular assessments to track progress.

  • Mathematics, Science, English & Urdu
  • Arts & physical education

Middle School (6 - 8)

Preparatory courses to build critical thinking and readiness for higher grades.

  • Project-based learning
  • Computer basics & research skills

Labs & IT

Hands-on learning through well-equipped labs and an IT curriculum that prepares students for a digital future.

Computer Lab

Modern computer lab with workstations for class-based instruction and coding clubs.

  • 20+ Windows PCs with office & educational software
  • Basic coding lessons (Scratch, block-based, introductory Python)
  • Internet access under supervised conditions

Science Lab

Practical experiments and demonstration equipment to reinforce classroom learning.

  • Basic chemistry & physics apparatus
  • Structured lab sessions aligned to the curriculum
  • Safety-first approach with trained instructors

IT Resources

School IT infrastructure and learning resources for both students and teachers.

  • Projector and AV systems in classrooms
  • Teacher training in digital lesson delivery
  • IT Helpdesk for student/device support
